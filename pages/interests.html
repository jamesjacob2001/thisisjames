About Me

My Interests

The things that keep me busy, excited, and always wanting to learn more.

01 — Sports

Boston Sports

As someone living in Boston, I enjoy following the city's rich sports culture. I keep up with teams like the Celtics, Patriots, Red Sox, and Bruins and appreciate the passion that Boston fans bring to every season. Sports are a great way to connect with the community and share exciting moments with friends and fellow fans.

02 — Motorsport

Formula 1

I became interested in Formula 1 because of the unique combination of speed, strategy, and cutting-edge engineering. What fascinates me most is how teams constantly innovate to gain even the smallest competitive advantage. I also enjoy following the rivalries, race-day decisions, and the teamwork that goes on behind the scenes.

03 — Music

Music & DJing

Music has always been one of my favorite ways to relax and stay inspired. I enjoy exploring different genres, from hip-hop and R&B to electronic and house music, depending on my mood. Beyond listening, I have developed an interest in DJing, which has given me a deeper appreciation for how music can bring people together and create memorable experiences.
